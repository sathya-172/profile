This Is My Career

Frontend Developer 2019 - Present

Over the last few years, I’ve had the chance to grow with two amazing teams — starting out as an enthusiastic developer and gradually stepping into a lead role in frontend development.

My journey in tech began back in 2019. I was working full-time, learning by doing, and building interfaces that people could actually use. In 2021, I shifted to part-time so I could focus on my degree — juggling projects and college at the same time. It wasn’t easy, but it taught me a lot about balance, discipline, and staying sharp under pressure.

Today, I work as a key frontend developer, building real-world applications in a fast-moving environment. I love shaping smooth user experiences, thinking through performance, and collaborating with other teams to bring ideas to life. For me, good UI is more than just visuals — it’s about clarity, flow, and making tech feel effortless for the user.
